Enterprise/Platform Clinical Review

Independent senior clinical judgment for organizations scaling standardized rehabilitation programs

Who This Is For

This service is designed for organizations that deliver rehabilitation at scale and need senior clinical perspective to identify risks before they multiply.

  • Employer benefits platforms offering MSK or rehab programs
  • Telehealth providers scaling standardized protocols
  • Insurance-adjacent wellness companies
  • Healthcare systems implementing digital rehabilitation

The Problem

Rehabilitation programs that work in controlled settings often fail when scaled. The clinical nuances that experienced therapists navigate intuitively—pain variability, adherence patterns, progression timing—become invisible at the platform level.

One flawed assumption, replicated across thousands of users, creates systematic risk. Most platforms discover these gaps reactively, through poor outcomes or user complaints.

A senior clinical review surfaces these risks proactively, providing documentation that your organization had the program reviewed by an independent expert before scaling further.

What the Review Includes

  • Program-level and protocol-level clinical analysis
  • Assessment of core clinical assumptions (pain, adherence, progression, variability)
  • Identification of risk zones and blind spots
  • Scaling concern analysis (what breaks at 100 users vs. 1,000+)
  • Written summary with concern levels and high-level recommendations

What This Is NOT

  • Patient care or medical advice
  • Outcome validation or efficacy testing
  • Certification or regulatory approval
  • Guarantee of results

This review reflects senior clinical judgment based on experience. It is designed to surface risks and assumptions, not to validate or certify your program.
