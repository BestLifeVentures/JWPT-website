App Clinical Review

Senior clinical judgment for digital rehab and physical therapy applications

Who This Is For

This service is designed for teams building digital rehabilitation products who need experienced clinical perspective before launch or scale.

  • Digital rehab and PT app developers
  • In-home MSK platform builders
  • Health tech teams with technical founders lacking clinical depth
  • Product teams preparing for clinical partnerships or pilots

The Problem

Rehab apps fail at scale because they encode clinical assumptions that don't hold in the real world. AI systems over-assume adherence, misread pain responses, and expect linear progression that rarely occurs.

Most app teams discover these flaws through user churn, negative reviews, or worseâ€”actual harm. Technical founders often lack the clinical intuition to anticipate where their logic will break.

A senior clinical review brings 30 years of real-world rehabilitation experience to your product before users encounter problems your team couldn't foresee.

What the Review Includes

  • Product-level clinical analysis of your app's rehabilitation approach
  • Assessment of embedded assumptions (pain models, adherence expectations, progression logic)
  • Identification of clinical blind spots and edge cases
  • Analysis of where human judgment is still required
  • Written feedback with prioritized concerns and recommendations

What This Is NOT

  • Patient care or medical advice
  • Outcome validation or clinical trial support
  • Certification, endorsement, or regulatory approval
  • Guarantee of clinical effectiveness

This review provides senior clinical judgment to help you identify risks. It does not validate your app's efficacy or serve as a clinical endorsement.
